Outcome

When reviewing the standard, you must:

  • ensure the standard can be applied in a binary way
  • ensure the standard is clear and unambiguous
  • ensure the standard is written using plain language and any acronyms, initialisms, and abbreviations are written out in full
  • check that there is no commercially sensitive or secret information included which could damage department reputation or put the department at risk
  • check that the standard is not a duplicate of another standard
