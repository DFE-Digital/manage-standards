Standards are rules or guidelines that need to be followed. You must meet the relevant standards to ensure the expected quality levels for your service.

By following a common set of standards, we ensure consistency across the department and give ourselves a framework where we can measure compliance.

Standards should be:

  • easy to access, so everyone understands what they need to include as part of their delivery needs and can plan for this
  • clear and simple, so it's easy to check if something meets a standard
  • flexible, so they can change over time, if needed, as we update and manage new standards
