Support

You can get help and support in the following ways:

  • Post a message in the #standards channel on DfE Slack
  • Email the standards forum at ddt.standards@education.gov.uk

Standards forum

The standards forum is a group of people from across the DDT directorate including leads and heads of profession.

The forum meets regularty to discuss and agree standards, and supports people wanting to develop standards with advice and guidance.
