You can get help and support in the following ways:

  • Post a message in the #standards channel on DfE Slack
  • Email the standards forum at ddt.standards@education.gov.uk

Help with a specific standard

You should contact the named owner or contacts on the standard for help and support in the first instance.

Standards forum

The standards forum is a group of people from across DDT including leads and heads of profession.

The forum meets regularly to discuss and review standards, and supports people wanting to develop standards with advice and guidance.
